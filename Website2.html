Outlook/Prognosis

Rabies is nearly always fatal for humans and animals once the characteristic neurological symptoms like hydrophobia and hallucinations develop. The rabies virus causes inflammation in the brain, leading to severe damage and ultimately death. The good news is that rabies is entirely preventable through vaccination and avoiding contact with infected animals. If you suspect rabies exposure (from bite or scratch), seeking medical attention immediately is crucial.
